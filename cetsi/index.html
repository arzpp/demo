WELCOME

CCI ELECTROMECH TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS, INC. (CETSI) is a duly licensed corporation granted by Securities and Exchange Commission bearing number CS201533641, pursuant to its Article of Incorporation: “Its primary purposes includes but not limited to: Testing and Commissioning of Electrical, Mechanical and Telecoms equipments and accessories, installation, surveys and all other engineering services.”

CETSI have expertise in the field of Electrical Engineering such as: Transmission Line, Distribution Line, Industrial Installation and various maintenance works; It is also competent in delivering high quality Switchgears, Panel Boards, Motor Control Centers as part of its Product Lines.

The Company is composed of a team, with extensive training, applied thru a long years in the actual service in the Industry. With the combined expertise and effort of all our personnel, we believe that we can deliver the services beyond expectation of our clients.

MISSION

CCI ELECTROMECH TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS, INC. (CETSI) is committed to serve the Nation with highest quality of services, manpower and supplies in all aspect of Engineering and Electrical undertakings.

VISION

(CETSI) aims to be recognized as the Country’s leading Engineering and Electrical Contractor that adheres to outmost standard of service excellence in delivering its commitment of full customer satisfaction.

Our Services

General electrical servicing and preventive maintenance.

Standard electrical and testing including protective relays and metering test calibration.

Supply and installation of low voltage dry type transformer and power capacitors (auto, semi auto and manual operated).

Design, supply, installation and commissioning of low voltage switchgear, power breakers, vacuum circuit breakers (VCB), air circuit breaker (ACB) and MCCB for general power and lighting system and power generating units.

Installation and commissioning of power transformer.

Supply, installation and commissioning of Electrical Motors both A/C and D/C and Variable Frequency Drive.

Supply, installation and commissioning of motor control center (MCC), panel boards, cable trays, bus trays, ATS, MTS and synchronizing panel.

Design, erection and installation of overhead/underground distribution lines, pole line hardwares and protection and pad mounted transformers.
